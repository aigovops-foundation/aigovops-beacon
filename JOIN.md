# Join

Three doors. Pick whichever fits.

## Use it

Stand Beacon up. The walkthrough takes about an afternoon:

- Live demo: [bobrapp.github.io/aigovops-beacon](https://bobrapp.github.io/aigovops-beacon/)
- Walkthrough: [bobrapp.github.io/aigovops-beacon/walkthrough](https://bobrapp.github.io/aigovops-beacon/walkthrough/)
- Starter zip: [downloads/aigovops-beacon-starter.zip](https://bobrapp.github.io/aigovops-beacon/downloads/aigovops-beacon-starter.zip)

When you hit something confusing, [open an issue](https://github.com/bobrapp/aigovops-beacon/issues/new). That is real contribution.

## Build it

Send a PR. The full guide is in [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md). The short version:

1. Fork.
2. Branch.
3. Small PR.
4. Be kind. See [`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

If you'd like to be in the review circle (see [`MEETINGS.md`](MEETINGS.md)), self-nominate by opening an issue with the title "review circle: self-nomination — [your name]" and a paragraph about what you'd bring. The steward and maintainers pick the rotating community seat together.

## Back it

If your company's work touches AI governance and you want to support the program:

- The standard the Foundation's projects implement is [OVERT 1.0](https://overt.is/), stewarded by [Glacis](https://www.glacis.io/). The right first conversation is with them.
- For the Foundation itself, reach out to [bob.rapp@aigovops.community](mailto:bob.rapp@aigovops.community) or [ken.johnston@aigovops.community](mailto:ken.johnston@aigovops.community).
- What sponsorship is and is not: see [`ENGAGEMENT.md`](ENGAGEMENT.md).

## What you won't find here

- Exclusive integrations.
- "Foundation-endorsed" badges for commercial products.
- A paywall around any community resource.
- A faster merge because of a sponsorship.

Those are habits we plan to keep.
