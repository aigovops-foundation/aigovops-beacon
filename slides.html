What you'll sign Section 4 of Level 100

An Ed25519 key. In your browser. Never leaves your tab.

The signature

A 64-byte Ed25519 signature over canonical JSON of the receipt. Sorted keys, no whitespace, UTF-8. The same canonicalization auditors will use to re-verify it later.

The bundle

A real downloadable zip. Manifest + receipts NDJSON + your public key + a VERIFY.md that walks an auditor through verifying with stock openssl. No Beacon installation required.
